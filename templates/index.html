My Journey

Strength training changed my life. I lost 20+ kg, improved my thyroid health, built real stamina, and feel stronger and leaner than ever. With 10+ years of experience and a Level 3 fitness certification, I now help women achieve the same.

Why strength training? It builds lean muscle, not bulk. It raises metabolism so you burn more fat at rest. It supports joints and lowers injury risk. It improves balance, posture, and bone density. It shapes your body in ways cardio alone cannot. It lifts mood, confidence, and energy.

The program is simple: 30 minutes, three times a week. Short, focused, and easy to stick to, designed for busy schedules without sacrificing results.

What you get: nutrition support, practical health guidance, occasional at-home sessions, regular measurement check-ins, and ongoing motivation and accountability — all at no extra cost.

Ready to get started? Message me to learn how the program works and how it can help you get stronger, leaner, and feel amazing.
